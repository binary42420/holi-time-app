Error: Request Header Fields Too Large

What happened? Your browser cookies have grown too large, causing the server to reject requests.

Quick Fix Options:

Option 1: Clear Cookies Only

This will log you out but preserve other data.

Option 2: Full Cleanup

This will clear cookies, localStorage, and cache.

Option 3: Manual Steps

  1. Press F12 to open DevTools
  2. Go to Application tab
  3. Click Storage in left sidebar
  4. Click "Clear site data"
  5. Go back to localhost:3000
